    ▼ About

    I was born in Denton, Texas. I am 15 years old and live in Oakland, California.

    School

    I am currently a freshman at MetWest High School. At school I take 6 different classes. I take Advisory which is more or less Humanities, History and English with a lot of Ethnic Studies. I preffer to think of this class as homeroom because that is what it feels like to me. I also take Integrated Science 1 which should prepare me for when I take Biology and Chemistry or other sciences. There's also Build, a class about entreprenuership and running your own business. For this class I am working on creating a mobile application called DreamTime with the help of Coach Wolf from CodeNext. I take also take IWT, Independent Work Time, which is a free block where I can do work and complete any homework I have. I take Geometry as well. Then there's Studio Production which is a Laney classs, in the community college. This class is a college class which counts as college credit and art credit. I hope that after taking that class I can get an internship related to film next year.

    CodeNext

    I am currently enrolled at CodeNext. CodeNext is a program which empowers youth of Black and Latinx communities with computer science skills and leadership skills. Making the next generation of tech leaders and innovators. I joined this program because I felt that I could use this opportunity to step out of the school enviornment and try something that I was into. At the time I didn't have much experience with STEAM, but I learned that there was so much more I could do esspecially with Computer Science. CodeNext has helped me understand new projects and concepts I didn't know of or think of before and I hope to keep learning more.

    Tech

    I love tech because with tech you can create so many things. I was able to make this portfolio because of tech. Tech has can change the world around you and lead to some interesting inovations. I hope that I can make a difference in this world with tech. I believe that with tech I could create a website, game or app. But most importantly I can use tech to change my community and make the World a better place.
